Programme information

Discover the key leadership skills required for social leadership and map your future leadership journey with our four week online introductory course.

The Discover Programme is a great first step or refresh for anyone working for social change who is looking to develop themselves as leaders in order to transform their organisations, communities and the world around them.

Course Detail
  • 2-4 hours/week
  • All levels of experience
  • £50
  • Certificate of completion
Course Features
  • 4-week course
  • Exclusively online
  • Self-paced learning
  • Peer networks

Presenters

Mikayla Sinead

Mikayla joins with a passion for leading with an inclusive intersectional mindset whilst centring joy. Mikayla’s career has focussed on developing more inclusive workplaces & future leaders. Mikayla now combines her love for gaming with professional experience through her company Bridge Network Group which focuses on boosting representation in organisations. Additionally, as a passionate gamer and content creator on the streaming platform Twitch, Mikayla is on a mission to build a community of Black Women and future changemakers in the video game industry.

Mikayla has a wealth of experience in supporting young and diverse talent to reach positions of influence and leadership. Mikayla is in the 2019 cohort of Birmingham Live’s 30 under 30 in recognition of her work with young people in the city. She also has experience with MAIA Group, TAG Network West Midlands, Amplify DEI, The British Council, UpRising Leadership West Midlands Combined Authority, Ultra Education, University of Birmingham, Enrol Yourself: The Learning Marathon, West Midlands Women's Voice, and iDNetwork. Mikayla is a confident public speaker and in 2018 spoke at the Women of the World Festival in Brazil.
